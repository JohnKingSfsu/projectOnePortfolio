What Inspires Me

By: John King

Colde -- Korean Musician



The first artist I thought of to talk about it Colde. Colde is a Korean musician and He inspires me because he makes his music and is very real about it. He's not making music to fit in or trying to stand out, he's just expressing himself and that inspires me to do the same in different ways. As well as this, the way he makes music and expresses his thoughts are so visual, it doesn't feel like just music, he is using sound to paint a picture which inspires me to imagine the place-setting of the song.


Yoonseul-Spotify

Nathanaël Bronn -- Character Designer



The next artist I would say inspires me is Nathanaël Bronn. Nathanaël Bronn is the art director and character designer for the show "Miraculous: Tales of Ladybug and Cat Noir". Even though I did not know who he was until I stared this assignment, he inspired me through the show without me even knowing. What inspires me about his work is how he managed to create characters that perfectly support their personalities and responsibilities. I think it is incredible to be able to create a visual based on information and roles, which is what he does.


Nathanael Bronn-Instagram

Olivia Rodrigo -- Musical Artist



The next person that has inspired me in a way is Olivia Rodrigo. She is a famous singer song-writer. What inspires me about her is that she found a way to express her thoughts in a very clear way, while also making them so aesthetically pleasing. I got very inspired by her talent for music as well as her ability to express herself in a way that was able to make a billboard list. Combining good music with expression is difficult to do but she does it.
